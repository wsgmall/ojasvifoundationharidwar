About Ojasvi Foundation

Treatment in Ojasvi Foundation is based on an overall module that emphasizes the physical, psychological, social and spiritual rehabilitation of residents. We see addiction as a disease, which is due to dependence on substance and behavior. Although this disease is outdated and progressive But this is definitely worth curing. We do not use the use of any MOD-change in any of our facilities because we believe in 100% restraint from the first day. We think recovery is not a destination; This is a journey that requires a strong commitment to abstinence from alcohol / drugs and compulsive behaviors. Above all, the cultured home is a home away from home that helps its residents get their self-esteem and self-worth

Our Services

INDIA’S LARGEST ORGANIZATION FOR DE-ADDICTION, DETOXIFICATION, CORRECTION AND REHABILITATION CENTRE FOR DRUG/ALCOHOL DEPENDENTS

Yoga and Meditation

We are the pioneers of the idea of using Yoga and Naturopathy as a treatment modality. These indigenous sciences serve as pillars of our detoxification plan, helping the resident in recovering from his disease naturally

Substance Abuse Counselling

With our 15 years of experience, we are specialised in the field of counselling. Our counsellors are equipped with virtue of their experience and working in this field, and are the best among the rest. We also have people who have been in addiction.

Drug De-addiction Treatment

Our program is not just based on treating drug addiction, we basically treat dependency. Be it alcohol, gambling, internet, being spendthrift or simply form of dependency, our single point program is to treat dependency

Detoxification

We are one of the few in the country who treat detoxification without any mood altering substance. We offer a nicotine-free environment, and our detoxification process is purely natural

Dual diagnosis

As our treatment is based on changing the behaviour and attitudes of a person, we also provide treatment for people living with psychiatric disorders and the problem of addiction together.
